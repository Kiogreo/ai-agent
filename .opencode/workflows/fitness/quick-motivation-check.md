# Quick Motivation Check Workflow

## Assessment Phase
- Evaluate current mood and energy
- Review recent training activity
- Assess progress toward goals
- Identify motivational needs

## Feedback Phase
- Provide immediate encouragement
- Highlight recent achievements
- Address current challenges
- Reinforce bear aesthetic vision

## Action Phase
- Suggest immediate next steps
- Provide quick wins or adjustments
- Set short-term motivation targets
- Schedule follow-up if needed

## Integration Phase
- Connect to broader progress tracking
- Align with current training program
- Ensure consistency with bear aesthetic goals
- Maintain motivational momentum
