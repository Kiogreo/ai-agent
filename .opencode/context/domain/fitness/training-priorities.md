# Training Priorities

## Primary Focus Areas
1. **Chest Development**: Bench press, incline press, flyes, push-ups
2. **Arm Development**: Bicep curls, tricep extensions, hammer curls
3. **Shoulder Development**: Overhead press, lateral raises, front raises
4. **Back Development**: Rows, pull-ups, deadlifts, face pulls
5. **Belly/Core**: Planks, Russian twists, leg raises, weighted crunches

## Secondary Priorities
- Leg development for balanced proportions
- Functional movement patterns
- Injury prevention exercises

## Exercise Selection Criteria
- Compound movements prioritized over isolation
- Exercises supporting bear aesthetic goals
- Safe execution with proper form
- Equipment availability considerations

## Progression Guidelines
- Increase weight when completing target reps with good form
- Add sets before increasing weight
- Focus on weekly progression rather than daily
- Allow deload weeks for recovery

## Recovery Considerations
- 48-72 hours between same muscle group training
- Sleep and nutrition as critical recovery factors
- Active recovery through light activity
- Stress management for optimal recovery
